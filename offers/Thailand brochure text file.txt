What You'll Experience
Our 5-day tour is designed to immerse you in the best that Pattaya has to offer:

Coral Island Tour (with Lunch): Journey across the turquoise waters to Coral Island. Spend your day swimming, sunbathing on white sandy beaches, or trying out exciting water sports. A delicious local lunch is included to complete your perfect island day.

The Alcazar Show: Prepare to be amazed by a spectacular performance at the Alcazar Cabaret. This world-renowned show features extravagant costumes, stunning performers, and mesmerizing set designs that will leave you breathless.

Tiger Park Visit: An exhilarating experience awaits you at the Tiger Park. Safely encounter these magnificent creatures in a clean and natural environment. It's a unique opportunity for incredible photos and lasting memories.

Pattaya City Tour: Discover the heart and soul of the city. Our tour will take you to key landmarks, offering insights into the local culture and lifestyle. The tour includes a visit to the famous Gems Gallery, where you can admire exquisite Thai jewelry and craftsmanship.
